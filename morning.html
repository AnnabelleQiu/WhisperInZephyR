ta

gua

After November, he became obsessed with a soccer game. He stopped answering calls or texts and basically vanished for months. I was speechless，but who am I? I never been someone who’s overly focused on relationships, so I shifted my attention back to my own life. You know The Sims 4, right? You asked for my account previously, yeah I started playing it again, initially just to keep myself busy to forget abt that stupid guy. Gradually, though, I found myself getting into independent creation. What began as casual gaming evolved into making my own game mods, doing 3D modeling and rendering, reprogramming game mechanics, and building my own website. Within a year, I was taking on projects and making six figures-ALL BY MYSELF.
That’s when I realized that the emotional value I get from others can’t even compare to the fulfillment of earning my own income.
